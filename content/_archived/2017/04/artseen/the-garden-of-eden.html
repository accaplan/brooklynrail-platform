--- title: The Garden of Eden ---

Heinz Mack

Sperone Westwater / February 17 – March 25, 2017

 

Symphony of light and refrains of machine unconscious Plummet this anomalous and unflustered Eden into Deliberations of unequal passages, And arousals of our senses, sense of …

The appreciable accumulation of an intense, unraveling duel Between symmetrical speckles, flecks, dabs and blotches, Smudged spots and cool asymmetry; a utopia of vertical pillars Shines against the tan desert sand.

Light is the seclusive lord of universal acceptance Preceding the mechanization of private folklore, Squeezed strenuously across transparent veils, filmic automatons, Onto rolls of radiant color.

The orchestra begins long before dawn. Someone whispers, “It’s too splendid to confess.” Time, space, elemental commitment is to nowhere But here. Everyone is welcomed.
