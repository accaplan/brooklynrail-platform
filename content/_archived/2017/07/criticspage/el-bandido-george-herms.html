              Short and sweet the skinny on Walter Hopps

 

Mystery and Power of Art

Lifetime spent in presenting

That which is beyond price:

The personal relationship between two people

His definition of art:

Art offers the possibility

of love with strangers.

 

A true artist he called me,

he had no higher compliment.

Thanks, Walter

Takes one to know one.
