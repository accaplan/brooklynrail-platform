--- title: Ben Keating ---

All we do is look
Down
any more
We need to look
Up

What sets an arts space apart is its ability to be a part of art embracing process in front and behind the event of art making.

A museum is the life and ecosystem of creativity, providing an estuary for artist to habitat, it’s this brackish ground between museums and studio, dance or foundry, intimate gallery or grand space—the ability to embrace everything in between.
