--- title: >- UGLY DUCKLING PRESSE i. Preface, Part I of Love, Delight, and Alarm ---

 

Along a plane I wanted to write it: Where one end is fixed, a loose sun
To the excess of dark I was certain That certainty’s a kind of excess
A term that’s but a navigating bust A place to version a limit, feet in foot
When the lute’s sum turns daily As if by steam one could coordinate
The tulips, too numerous to pinch By steam the autography of material
Will appear to light the vapor region So we can know it as if by touch
A nude thing, the masses say As they re-substantiate again
