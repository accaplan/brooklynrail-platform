--- title: DAVID HINTON ---

 

 

Thought gone
dark needs nothing more of it- self, needs dark
alone woven ink- black through blackened light. It’s an ancient tool, this gathering—
basket of shadow, and it glistens still with
use. Hummingbird’s quick glint, crow’s winged fleck of night: these last few things
slip vanishing through, leaving again this dark harvest of origins.
