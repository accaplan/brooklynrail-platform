--- title: After Rilke ---

 

Someday, when will I lose you? Will you be able to sleep without me? Will you hear me whispering in your dream? Like a star-anise growing next to you?

My words are your eyelids Do your breasts listen? Will your love listen like a melissa kissing my eyelids with your mouth?

We both close and leave We’re both one but are each other’s Like a garden that can kiss Like the kiss that comes before more kisses
