Borough President

The seventh branch reached the third floor and flowered outside my window did I notice? the glass was dirty or I had a lazy eye  but with a flush of cheek it changed try not to get near it with words were you on the S.I. ferry going home? it’s Saturday but the assholes can’t take my apartment away from me can they? the city runs backwards the rivers are zeros the trees are ones the corner hibiscus in full bloom ready for romance I’d like some anything running counter to the plan have you disembarked yet? are you flashing your horn? picking a fight discussing food with someone who reminds you of your grandmother Rita?

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Held Together by My Jacket
